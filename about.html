About SparkleClean

Your trusted partner in professional cleaning since 2015

Our Story

SparkleClean was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide professional, reliable, and affordable cleaning services to families and businesses in our community. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted name in cleaning excellence.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, recognized a gap in the market for trustworthy cleaning services that prioritize quality and customer satisfaction. Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers and continue to expand our services.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional cleaning services that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and environmental responsibility.
